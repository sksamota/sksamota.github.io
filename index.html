Who I Am

I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. at the Department of Mathematics, National Institute of Technology Rourkela, one of India's premier technical institutions.

Under the guidance of Prof. Snehashish Chakraverty, my doctoral research lies at the intersection of mathematics and artificial intelligence ‚Äî specifically in leveraging machine learning models to solve dynamical and differential equation problems that arise in science and engineering.
